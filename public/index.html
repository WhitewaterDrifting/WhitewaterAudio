About

Hi, I'm Adam — an Audio Engineer from South Wales with a passion for bringing stories to life through sound. I specialise in Dialogue Editing, Mixing, and Sound Design, and I'm always up for a new challenge or creative adventure.

I've had the privilege of collaborating with a wide range of talented creatives on projects of all shapes and sizes. Most recently, I worked on the documentary The Red List, which screened at this year's Raindance Festival in June.
